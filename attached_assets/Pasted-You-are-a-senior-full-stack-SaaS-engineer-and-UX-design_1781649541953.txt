You are a senior full-stack SaaS engineer and UX designer.

Build a complete production-ready web application called "DevDonna Outreach".

## Goal

The application should allow me to:

1. Upload a Google Sheet export (CSV or XLSX).
2. Store and manage business contacts.
3. Filter contacts by category.
4. Write one email template.
5. Send the email to all businesses within a selected category.
6. Track email sending status.

## Target User

A solo consultant using the platform for B2B outreach and lead generation.

---

## Core Features

### 1. Import Contacts

Create an upload page where I can upload:

* CSV files
* XLSX files

Example columns:

* Company Name
* Contact Name
* Email
* Website
* Industry
* Category
* City
* Country
* Notes
* Phone Number

The system should automatically map columns.

Store all imported contacts in a database.

---

### 2. Contacts Dashboard

Create a modern dashboard showing:

* Total contacts
* Total categories
* Total emails sent
* Total pending contacts

Include:

* Search bar
* Filters
* Sorting

Display contacts in a table.

Columns:

* Company
* Contact
* Email
* Category
* Website
* Status

---

### 3. Category Management

Allow me to:

* Create categories
* Edit categories
* Delete categories

Examples:

* E-commerce
* SaaS
* Construction
* Healthcare
* Real Estate

A contact can belong to one category.

---

### 4. Email Campaign Builder

Create a page where I can:

Select:

* One category

Write:

* Subject line
* Email body

Use variables such as:

{{company_name}}

{{contact_name}}

{{website}}

{{industry}}

Example:

Hello {{contact_name}},

I noticed that {{company_name}} has a strong presence in the {{industry}} industry.

---

### 5. Email Sending

Integrate with:

* Resend API (preferred)
* SMTP as alternative

Requirements:

* Send personalized emails to every contact in selected category.
* Replace variables dynamically.
* Queue emails safely.
* Prevent duplicate sends.

---

### 6. Email Tracking

Show:

* Sent
* Failed
* Pending

Track:

* Date sent
* Recipient
* Campaign

Create a campaign history page.

---

### 7. Contact Details Page

Clicking a contact should open a detailed page displaying:

* Company information
* Contact information
* Notes
* Email history
